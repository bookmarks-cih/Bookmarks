01. Recent Updates

Project Changelog

Track the evolution of Bookmarks with our latest feature releases, performance improvements, and community announcements.

March 9, 2026

New Sections: Civil Rights & Psychology

We are excited to announce the addition of two new sections to the Docs area of Bookmarks: "Civil Rights" and "Psychology". These categories have been carefully curated to provide valuable resources and documentation on important social justice and mental health topics. This update reflects our ongoing commitment to expanding access to knowledge.

March 8, 2026

Performance Overhaul & Special Wishes

We have significantly improved the homepage and the hub by reducing animation lag and simplifying navigation on mobile. To support these enhancements, we have integrated new libraries such as AlpineJS, ThreeJS, GSAP, and AnimeJS.

Additionally, we have completed the graphic redesign of our HTTPing, however this service will be paused for the foreseeable future due to a lack of support. On a lighter note, we wish a joyful International Women's Day 2026 to all the women in our community.

March 6, 2026

Dump Status Dashboard & CSV Access

We are excited to announce the launch of our new Dump Status page, a dedicated dashboard designed to provide full transparency on the health of the Bookmarks repository. You can now monitor the status of thousands of links in real-time.

For developers, we have also made the entire archive accessible via CSV files on GitHub.

May 22, 2025

Wellness & Visual Identity Update

The Bookmarks team is excited to announce the addition of the Safety & Prevention category, designed to provide curated resources on mental health, well-being, and personal safety.

We’ve also rolled out significant visual updates to the main dashboard, refining the atmosphere with layered gradients and improving the "glassmorphism" effects on cards for better readability.
