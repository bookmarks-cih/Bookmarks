Become a featured voice.

Apply to appear in the Bookmarks Creators directory. Share your work, your sector, and the external resources that help your community understand your universe better. Bookmarks highlights creators we judge as quality voices with useful context, real signal, and something worth discovering.

More visibility Get a clean public profile designed to help new people discover your work.
Resource context Add links, tools, references, videos, articles, podcasts, or docs connected to your field.
Quality signal Bookmarks is selective: Creator profiles are meant for voices with clarity, usefulness, and taste.

Why become a Creator?

A Creator profile is not just a link page. It is a curated identity card for your work, your references, and the resources that make your niche easier to explore.

Appear inside the Creators tab with a clean public profile.
Help your audience discover your best resources without noise.
Connect with people in the same domain through better attribution and discovery.

How the review works

Creator submissions are reviewed manually. The goal is to keep the directory focused, useful, and aligned with the Bookmarks quality standard.
