Why it matters

A map of trusted internet ecosystems.

Bookmarks becomes a place where expertise, taste, context, and identity shape discovery — more human, more niche, and far more memorable than a plain directory.

Human context

Links feel stronger when users understand why they were selected and who stands behind them. Stacks provide narrative, trust, and editorial framing.

Better discovery

A person-driven browsing model helps users reach precise subcultures and useful niches more quickly than broad taxonomy alone ever could.

Creator value

It gives creators a clean public page for their best resources, with attribution, direct traffic, and a better way to organize useful internet.

Designed for human collaboration, not lazy scraping.

Bookmarks features resources through voluntary contribution, curator-approved selections, and assisted curation workflows that stay respectful, editable, and clearly attributed.

Official attributionEvery collection carries curator credit and profile links.
Flexible inputExports, lists, docs, spreadsheets, or rough drafts — all workable.
Editorial controlCurators can request edits, updates, or removal at any time.
